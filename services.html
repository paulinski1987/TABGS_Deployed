Services Offered

Want to update the look of your bathroom? There are few additions you can make that transform your bathroom as drastically as the addition of a glass shower. Whether building a new house or renovating your existing washroom, installing a new glass shower will give your facilities a sleek and clean modern look. Did I mention glass is easier to keep clean than ceramic?

Building a new house, renovating your existing house, or repairing damage from a baseball from the neighborhood children, we can do it all. We can install prefabricated units complete with rfaming and finishing or we can build the window by hand using modern aluminum framing or classic wood. If you like your existing framing we can replicate it so it matches the rest of your windows

A leaking window frame can cause significant damage to your home. Water that has leaked in can damage your walls, degrade the framing or even cause mold to grow inside the walls. Removing old material and applying new caulking at the first sign of a leak will save you time, money and stress.

Glass doors have become popular in office buildings, store fronts, and houses. We can install traditional swinging doors as well as sliding doors depending on the setting. All door panes are made with tempered or laminated glass which are safe and secure.

Glass railings can be installed anywhere a traditional wood railing could. They can be used on interior or exterior staircases, porches, patios, balconies and terraces. While they are very stylish glass railings are extremely safe, often sturdier than their wood counterparts. All railings use strong safety glass that can withstand significant force.

Is your specific project not listed above? Never fear, our skilled glaziers can handle it. Give us a call or email with your idea and we will come discuss and assess the project free of charge.
